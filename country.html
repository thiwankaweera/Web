Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka, officially known as the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, is an island nation located in South Asia, situated in the Indian Ocean to the southwest of the Bay of Bengal and to the southeast of the Arabian Sea. It is separated from the Indian subcontinent by the Gulf of Mannar and the Palk Strait. The capital and largest city of Sri Lanka is Colombo.

India

India, officially the Republic of India, is a diverse and populous country in South Asia. Known for its rich history, cultural heritage, and geographic variety, India is the seventh-largest country by land area and the second-most populous in the world. Its capital is New Delhi. India's landscape includes the Himalayan mountain range, fertile plains along the Ganges river, and a vast Deccan plateau.

Pakistan

Pakistan, situated in South Asia, is a country with a rich tapestry of history, culture, and geographical diversity. Established in 1947 following the partition of British India, it shares borders with India to the east, Afghanistan and Iran to the west, and China to the north. Islamabad serves as its capital.

Bangladesh

Bangladesh, located in South Asia, is a densely populated and culturally rich country. Bordered by India to the west, north, and east, Myanmar to the southeast, and the Bay of Bengal to the south, it is characterized by a flat and fertile delta formed by the confluence of several major rivers, including the Ganges and Brahmaputra. Dhaka, the capital and largest city, is a bustling metropolis. The majority of the population speaks Bengali, and Islam is the predominant religion.
