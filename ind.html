The country has a multiplicity of languages, religions, and ethnicities, with major religions including Hinduism, Islam, Christianity, Sikhism, and Buddhism. India has a mixed economy with significant growth in sectors like information technology, manufacturing, and services. The country has a parliamentary democratic system, and its history is marked by ancient civilizations, empires, and British colonial rule until gaining independence in 1947. India is renowned for landmarks such as the Taj Mahal, diverse wildlife, and a vibrant cuisine featuring dishes like curry and biryani.
